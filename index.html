Kevin Ahmetaj: Top 500 Albums of All Time

Description:

Our project displays data from the Top 500 Albums of All Time according to Rate Your Music. One page displays this in a filterable chart showing album title, artist, year, RYM rating and rank, and genres. The other page shows a customizable chart that allows users to compare frequencies of genres in the chart, or compare frequencies of albums in different decades.


How it Works

Our project is formatted as a website with 3 pages, accessible above: a home page and the two pages dedicated to viewing the data in different ways mentioned above. On the "Top 500 Albums" page, you can select a filter and type in a keyword to display a chart with every album that contains that keyword. On the "Graphing" page, you can either select "Decade" which shows a chart of album frequency over various decades, or "Genre" which allows you to customize a list of genres to compare frequencies.
